About the Book

Atomic Habits by James Clear reveals how small, consistent changes lead to remarkable outcomes. This site is designed to spread awareness about the core principles in the book.

Atomic Habits Summary

The book is based on the idea that habits are the compound interest of self-improvement. It introduces the 4 Laws of Behavior Change: make it obvious, make it attractive, make it easy, and make it satisfying.

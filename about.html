About James Clear

James Clear is a writer, speaker, and behavioral science expert. He’s best known for his international bestseller Atomic Habits, a book that has helped millions build better habits and improve their lives through small, consistent changes.

With a background in biomechanics and a deep passion for performance science, James brings together cutting-edge research and real-world application. His work focuses on how habits are formed, sustained, and optimized, and he presents it in ways that anyone can understand and apply.

James writes one of the most popular productivity newsletters on the internet and has been featured in major outlets including The New York Times, Time, Forbes, and CNBC. His insights extend beyond books — he’s widely followed for his quotes, cheat sheets, and downloadable PDF summaries available in English and Spanish (Hábitos Atómicos).

Whether through his audiobook, articles, or public speaking, James Clear continues to shape the conversation around behavior change, identity, and human potential.
